# Filters

## Recency

Supported values:

- `hour`
- `day`
- `week`
- `month`
- `year`

## Domain Filters

Pass a comma-separated list to `--domains`.

Examples:

- `developer.mozilla.org,w3.org`
- `-reddit.com`

Do not mix allow-list and deny-list domains in the same call.

## Search Modes

- `web`
- `academic`
- `sec`
